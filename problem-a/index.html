Bowser

Bowser, also known as King Koopa, is the main antagonist in the Super Mario series. This character is famed for his perpetual kidnapping of Princess Peach and his ultimate goal of conquering the Mushroom Kingdom.

  • His tenacity. Despite countless defeats by Mario, Bowser never gives up on his goals.
  • The complexity of his character. Bowser can play the villain but also shows moments of genuine care, especially towards his son, Bowser Jr.
  • His iconic design. Bowser's appearance is immediately recognizable and has become symbolic of the challenges faced by Mario.
